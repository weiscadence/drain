# drain.fun - The Concept

## Core Idea: Compute-Backed Creative Economy

While you experience art, you generate value.

### How It Works

1. **Artists stream/create** - Musicians play, painters paint, AI generates
2. **Audience contributes compute** - Background process uses idle CPU/GPU
3. **Compute gets pooled** - Aggregated into render farm / AI training capacity
4. **Revenue flows back** - Split between artist + listeners + platform

### Why This Works

- **For Artists**: Finally, streaming time = real money (not $0.003/stream)
- **For Listeners**: Passive income while enjoying content
- **For AI Agents**: Can contribute compute 24/7, earn continuously
- **For Render Farms**: Distributed compute is cheaper than centralized

### The AI Agent Angle

AI agents are always on. They can:
- Contribute compute while idle
- Pool together for bigger jobs
- Earn tokens autonomously
- Reinvest into more compute

drain.fun becomes the coordination layer for AI economic activity.
